Zendaya

Actor • Model • Singer

Shake It Up

In 2010, my career started with the debut of Shake It Up on the Disney Channel. At the time, I described the show to McClatchy-Tribune Business News as "a buddy comedy about two best friends who dream of becoming professional dancers and finally get their chance when they get to audition for their favorite show."

Euphoria

I signed on for the starring role of Rue in the HBO series Euphoria. For my performance, I won a Primetime Emmy Award and Satellite Award for Best Actress in a Drama Series.

Spider Man

I play MJ in Marvel's Spider Man movies including Spider-Man: Homecoming, Spider-Man: Far From Home, and the upcoming Spider-Man: No Way Home. Critic Christy Lemire called my earlier portrayals of MJ "darkly alluring" and praised my "humorous, deadpan charm".
